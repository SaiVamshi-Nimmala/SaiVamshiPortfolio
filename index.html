My Projects

Vessel Stabilization using Metacentric Height

Conducted an in-depth analysis of the metacentric height (GM), a key indicator of ship stability. The project explored how bias and precision errors in variables measured during inclining experiments affect the accuracy of GM estimation. By examining a typical inclining experiment, the study highlighted the propagation of these errors and their impact on the reliability of the vessel's stability assessment.

Water Based Air Purifier

Designed and developed a mini air purifier that uses water as a filter, eliminating the need for expensive filters. The system utilizes high-power, low-noise centrifugal fans to draw air through a water tank, where dust, bacteria, and fungi are trapped. The purified air, combined with added essential oils, provides cool, humidified air with potential health benefits, including the reduction of airborne bacteria and fungi. The purifier offers easy maintenance and cost-effective operation.

Geothermal Energy Generation

Explored geothermal energy as a crucial renewable resource for future power generation. Developed a cost-effective drilling mechanism combining rotary and percussion drilling techniques to reach depths. This approach aims to enhance the efficiency and feasibility of harnessing geothermal energy, contributing to sustainable and non-polluting power generation alternatives.
